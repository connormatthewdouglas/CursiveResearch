# Detailed Validation Records

The normal research workflow is now maintained through root
[`VALIDATION.md`](../VALIDATION.md) and [`CHANGELOG.md`](../CHANGELOG.md).

The files in this directory preserve earlier detailed validation work and may be
used for high-risk or technically involved investigations. They are no longer
required for routine chapter edits or research corrections.

Do not update the detailed ledger for ordinary changes unless the extra record
is specifically useful. Keep the concise status in `VALIDATION.md` current
instead.
